Web development nowadays.

I want to speak out. What do you imagine, when you hear the word "design"? Design is about creating something that people might like. What about "art"? Pretty tough, right? For me, "Art" is talking in a different manner. Not with your brain, but more with your soul.

So now: out of these two, which one is closer to "Web development"? Think about it. Kind of obvious it's "design", but why is it? This is what I'm trying to understand.

Well, because it's business after all! And most of the business is soulless. Typically, making a website is more about marketing and trying to sell something, than it is about giving up your soul to the audience.
